An Outline of the Rules

  1. The players or teams start on opposite sides of the net. One player is designated the server, and the opposing player is the receiver.
  2. The choice to be server or receiver in the first game and the choice of ends is decided by a coin toss before the warm-up starts. Service alternates game by game between the two players or teams.
  3. For each point, the server starts behind the baseline, between the center mark and the sideline. The receiver may start anywhere on their side of the net.
  4. When the receiver is ready, the server will serve, although the receiver must play to the pace of the server.
